I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in the Department of Informatics at King's College London.

Prior to that, I was a Lecturer in the School of Computing and Mathematical Sciences at Birkbeck, University of London. Earlier, I was a postdoctoral fellow in the Efi Arazi School of Computer Science at Reichman University (IDC Herzliya) hosted by Shay Mozes and Oren Weimann (University of Haifa).

I completed my Ph.D. studies in Computer Science in the Department of Informatics at King's College London under the supervision of Tomasz Radzik and Solon P. Pissis. During my Ph.D. studies, I completed a 6-month research internship in the Institute of Informatics at the University of Warsaw hosted by Jakub Radoszewski and a 5-month research internship at IDC Herzliya.

Research Interests

I am interested in algorithms and data structures, with a focus on strings and planar graphs.
