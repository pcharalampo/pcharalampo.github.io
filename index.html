I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science in the Efi Arazi School of Computer Science at the Interdisciplinary Center Herzliya under the supervision of Shay Mozes (IDC Herzliya) and Oren Weimann (University of Haifa).

Prior to that, I was a Ph.D. student in Computer Science in the Department of Informatics at King's College London under the supervision of Tomasz Radzik and Solon P. Pissis.

From October 2019 to March 2020 I was on a research internship in the Institute of Informatics at the University of Warsaw under the supervision of Jakub Radoszewski, partially supported by grant TOTAL.

From April 2018 to September 2018 I was on a research internship in the Efi Arazi School of Computer Science at the Interdisciplinary Center Herzliya under the supervision of Shay Mozes (IDC Herzliya) and Oren Weimann (University of Haifa).

I served on the PC of the 31st Annual Symposium on Combinatorial Pattern Matching (CPM 2020).
